Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Question Answer
Do you have baked goods ready? YES, we have fresh pastries and desserts ready on the fly!
How far in advance should I make a custom order? For custom orders, please give us a week to ensure quality of the product, espcially cakes
Do you Deliver? Unfortunately, we do NOT deliver but you're welcome to call in for a pickup!
What payment methods do you accept? Visa, credit cards and cash are all accepted
What happens once I place my custom order? Once the order is finalized and put in, we will work with you on the design closely to ensure it's perfection!
When's the next seasonal recipe? Our seasonal Recipes are on a fixed cycle throughout the year. It goes as follows:
  • Spring: March 1st - May 31st
  • Summer: June 1st - August 31st
  • Fall: September 1 - November 30th
  • Winter: December 1st - February 28th
Do you do online ordering? As of this time, we do NOT do online ordering for our products. Give us a call or see us in person and we'd be happy to help!
